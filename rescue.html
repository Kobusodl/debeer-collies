At De Beer Collies & Chocolate Pups, rescue is something deeply personal to us.

Over the years, we have opened our hearts and home to dogs in need — giving abandoned, neglected and displaced Border Collies and Australian Shepherds a second chance at the life they deserve.

As ethical breeders, we believe our responsibility goes beyond raising puppies. We believe in protecting, helping and advocating for these incredible breeds throughout every stage of their lives.

Whether through rescue, rehabilitation, fostering, support or helping dogs find safe forever homes — we want to make a difference where we can.
