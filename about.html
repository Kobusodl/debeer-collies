About Us

🐾

We are a small, dedicated breeder in Gauteng, passionate about raising exceptional Border Collies and Australian Shepherds with love, care and purpose.

Home raised. Heart loved.

☘ Our Story

Our Passion. Our Purpose.

Our love for dogs began many years ago with our first Border Collie. Their intelligence, loyalty and eagerness to please inspired us to learn more, do better and one day start our own breeding program.

Over time, Australian Shepherds became part of our journey — adding their affectionate nature, versatility and stunning beauty to our family.

Today, every puppy we raise is a reflection of our commitment to quality, health and lifelong companionship.

🐾 Our Breeding Philosophy
